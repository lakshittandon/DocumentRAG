Evaluation for document question answering should include retrieval metrics such as Recall at k, Mean Reciprocal Rank, and nDCG.
Answer quality should measure correctness, citation correctness, refusal behavior on negative queries, and hallucination rate.
A strong benchmark should contain single-hop questions, synthesis questions, and negative questions where the answer is not in the corpus.
Error analysis is as important as aggregate metrics because it reveals missing metadata, poor chunk boundaries, or weak reranking.
