Reliable retrieval-augmented generation combines information retrieval with constrained answer generation.
In a grounded RAG system, the model answers questions only from retrieved evidence rather than relying on unsupported memory.
Hybrid retrieval often combines dense vector retrieval with BM25 keyword retrieval to improve recall across semantic and lexical matches.
Reciprocal Rank Fusion is a simple but effective way to merge ranked lists from multiple retrievers.
Reranking improves precision by rescoring a small candidate set before generation.
Citation-aware answers improve trust because users can inspect the source passage, page, or section that supports the response.
